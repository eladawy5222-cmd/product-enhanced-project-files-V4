# marketing, sales, and crm playbook

Use this reference for lead generation, ad funnels, sales scripts, whatsapp scripts, crm follow-up, offers, segmentation, and campaign planning.

## tourism funnel model

1. awareness: destination inspiration, reels, guides, blogs, ads.
2. interest: trip page, itinerary download, whatsapp inquiry, price question.
3. consideration: compare inclusions, dates, company trust, reviews, safety.
4. booking: confirm availability, collect details, payment/reservation.
5. pre-trip: reminders, packing list, pickup details, support.
6. post-trip: review, referral, upsell next trip.

## lead qualification fields

Capture these in crm, airtable, forms, or chat:

- name
- phone/whatsapp
- preferred destination
- travel date or flexibility
- number of travelers
- trip type: private/group/family/corporate
- budget range
- city of departure
- language preference
- main concern or special request
- lead source
- status: new, contacted, qualified, proposal sent, follow-up, booked, lost
- next follow-up date

## whatsapp or phone sales flow

1. greet warmly and confirm destination interest.
2. ask 2-4 qualifying questions.
3. recommend the best package or ask permission to send options.
4. summarize value: itinerary, inclusions, safety, support, price/from.
5. handle objections.
6. create urgency only when true.
7. close with a clear next step: deposit, form, payment link, call, or itinerary confirmation.
8. log lead status and next follow-up.

## discovery questions

- تحبوا الرحلة تكون في أي تاريخ؟
- عدد الأفراد كام؟
- تفضلوا جروب ولا رحلة خاصة؟
- نقطة التحرك منين؟
- في ميزانية معينة حابين نمشي عليها؟
- الأهم بالنسبة لكم: السعر، الراحة، الفندق، البرنامج، ولا الخصوصية؟

English equivalents:

- what date are you considering?
- how many travelers will join?
- do you prefer a group tour or a private trip?
- where will you be starting from?
- do you have a preferred budget range?
- what matters most: price, comfort, hotel level, itinerary, or privacy?

## common objections and response angles

- price is high: compare inclusions, safety, comfort, service, hidden costs, and flexibility.
- need to think: offer a concise summary and follow-up time.
- not sure about date: suggest flexible dates or upcoming departures.
- worried about safety: mention transport, guides, support, reviews, and clear plan.
- comparing competitors: focus on transparent inclusions, service quality, and proof.
- group not confirmed: offer hold option if available or flexible plan.

## campaign planning template

For any campaign, define:

- objective: leads, bookings, awareness, repeat sales.
- audience: persona and location.
- offer: destination, duration, price/from, dates, inclusions.
- angle: family, budget, adventure, luxury, holiday, corporate.
- channels: meta, google search, tiktok, email, whatsapp, organic social.
- creative: headline, hook, visual direction, proof.
- landing page or whatsapp flow.
- crm handling: owner, status, follow-up schedule.
- metrics: cost per lead, qualified lead rate, booking rate, revenue, roas.

## follow-up timing

- instant: respond within minutes when possible.
- same day: send itinerary and answer key questions.
- next day: follow up with availability or useful info.
- 3 days later: ask if they want alternative dates or package.
- before departure deadline: remind only if real seats/deadline exists.
- after trip: review request and referral prompt.
