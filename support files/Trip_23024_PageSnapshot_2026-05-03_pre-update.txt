SECTION: Day Itinerary
1) Begin your journey at the National Museum of Egyptian Civilization, where you'll discover 50,000 ancient artifacts and stand before Egypt's pharaohs in the Royal Mummies Hall. Connect directly with ancient rulers in a powerful, unforgettable experience that brings history to life. (Approx. 2.5 hours)
2) Explore Saladin's 850-year-old fortress, uncovering breathtaking panoramic views of Cairo from the ancient ramparts. Capture perfect photos and gain spatial understanding of the city's layout while learning about medieval Islamic architecture. (Approx. 1.5 hours)
3) Savor authentic Egyptian cuisine during a traditional lunch at a local restaurant, experiencing warm Egyptian hospitality through classic dishes. Recharge with delicious local flavors that fuel your afternoon exploration. (Approx. 1 hours)
4) Journey to Old Cairo's historic heart to explore Coptic landmarks, including the serene Hanging Church and quiet courtyards. Discover the diverse spiritual traditions that shaped Cairo's identity through centuries of Christian heritage. (Approx. 1.5 hours)
5) Navigate Khan El-Khalili's labyrinthine alleys to discover hidden artisan workshops and shop for unique local treasures. Experience the vibrant energy of Cairo's most famous market while finding perfect souvenirs at your own pace. (Approx. 1 hours)
6) Relax during your comfortable return transfer as you reflect on a day filled with ancient wonders and cultural discoveries. End your journey refreshed with memories of Cairo's layered history and newfound understanding. (Approx. 0.5 hours)
